## Postdoctoral positions
Open positions will be announced here.

The group is generally supportive of candidates who wish to apply for their own postdoctoral funding to join the lab. If you are interested in this, you are welcome to get in touch by email to discuss potential projects and applications.

Relevant funding schemes include:
- **Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowships**  
  Highly competitive, but among the most prestigious and well-funded postdoctoral programmes in Europe.  
  [Link](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships)

- **Wenner-Gren Foundations Postdoctoral Scholarship**  
  A two-year research stipend for international researchers to carry out postdoctoral work in Sweden.  
  [Link](https://www.swgc.org/postdoktorsstipendier-sverige)

<br>

## PhD positions
Open positions will be announced here.

PhD positions in the group are typically tied to specific funded projects. At present, there is no general funding available for unsolicited PhD recruitment.

<br>

## Master’s thesis projects
Students enrolled at Linköping University who are interested in carrying out their master’s thesis in the group are encouraged to get in touch by email.

The group offers a broad range of possible projects, spanning experimental work, data analysis, and AI-driven materials research, with opportunities to tailor projects based on individual interests.
